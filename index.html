Tips For a Healthier Lifestyle

They may not seem big, but a little goes a long way!

6 Meals a Day

While it's been studied that this does not improve your metabolism, it is proven to help you control overeating.

Stop Eating Out

With the endless liquid calories, add-ons, and oversized portions, you're consuming more than you think.

Breakfast

Skipping breakfast hinders weight loss. It also refuels our body and recharges the brain.

Sports

Play your favorite sports! Don't have one? Simply walking for at least 30 minutes a day improves our health.

Sleep

Not only does sleeping banish our under eye-circles, but it improves our memory, promotes longevity, spur creativity, and sharpens our attention.

Daily Coffee = NO

Continuous coffee consumption causes adrenal exhaustion and chronic fatigue. Coffee also affects our stress level & metabolism.
